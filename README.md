# 🐈 About Me :
🧑‍💼 Statistician and data scientist at Dares, Statistics department of the French Labour Ministery.
Mainly working on labour conditions impacts on health using the *Conditions de Travail* survey and *SNDS* data (Medical consumption administrative data from French Social Security). I worked on determining eligibility for the work-related hardship scheme (*C2P*) based on *DSN* (monthly payroll reporting) data. Currently, I am working on updating the [*Conditions de travail* survey dataviz](https://dares.travail-emploi.gouv.fr/donnees/35-ans-devolutions-des-conditions-de-travail).

🏢 Member of @InseeFr civil servants.

🌐 Member of the [Institut Santé Numérique en Société](https://isns.fr/membre/leopold-maurice/) (Numeric Heath in Society Institute).

📈 Rstats enthousiast. 

💾 Advocating in favor of opensource.

🗺️ Love interactive dataviz, in particular maps.

🍵 Tea, ☕ coffee, 🧉 yerba mate addict.
